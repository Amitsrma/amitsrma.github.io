In developing countries, there is wide spread corruption and thus a lot of mis managed resources.
Reason is unaccountability bred through negligence.
There are institutions to check the negligence and un-ethical behavior but they themselves are marred by negligence, incompetence and greed.
This causes raft of public inconveniences.

Private oversight institutions are a possible solution but they burn through money, they are not profitable and hence not sustainable.

So, would it be possible to crowd source the oversight?

What does it mean?
We are all familiar with pranksters. They capture videos of un-noticing passers by.
Crowd-sourced oversight could be thought similarly. In this case un-noticing violaters would be captured in the video.
This should be effective because it would not cost money. The people who are wronged will be capturing video.
But how will the video be authenticated and the person doing wrong in video be brought to justice.
